Education

2021

High School

High school is a crucial time in a young person's life. During these four years, students typically range from ages 14 to 18. They take a variety of academic courses to prepare for the next stage of their education or career. High school provides opportunities for students to explore their interests through extracurricular activities, discover their passions, and develop important life skills.

2023

university

University is the next step for many high school graduates. At the university level, students typically range from ages 18 to 22. They choose a major field of study and take more specialized courses to develop expertise in that area. University offers a chance for greater independence and personal growth, as students manage their own schedules, live on their own, and make important decisions about their future.

2021

Internship

Internships are valuable work experience opportunities, often taken by university students or recent graduates. Internships allow people to gain practical, hands-on experience in a field or industry they are interested in pursuing as a career. Interns usually work part-time or full-time for a limited period, such as a semester or summer.

2021

job 1

Finding a job is an important milestone for many people after completing their education. Jobs provide the primary source of income that allows individuals to live independently and support themselves financially. The job market offers a wide variety of career paths and work environments, from corporate offices to small businesses, government agencies to non-profit organizations, and everything in between.
